Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how CS Downloader ("we", "our", or "us") handles your privacy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how we handle your information.

Data Collection

CS Downloader does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Our application:

Local Storage

Any data generated during the use of CS Downloader is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers. You maintain complete control over this data and can delete it at any time by removing the application.

Third-Party Services

CS Downloader does not integrate with any third-party services or analytics platforms. We do not share any information with third parties because we do not collect any information to share.
